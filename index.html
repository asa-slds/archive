About SLDS Conference

The SLDS Conference is the flagship event of the ASA Section on Statistical Learning and Data Science (SLDS). Started in 2016 and scheduled biannually, it was interrupoted by the COVID-19 pandemic but resumed in 2024.

  • Conference on Statistical Learning and Data Science (SLDS) 2016
    • University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, June 6 - 8, 2016
  • Conference on Statistical Learning and Data Science / Nonparametric Statistics (SLDS) 2018
    • Columbia University, June 4 - 6, 2018
  • Conference on Statistical Learning and Data Science / Nonparametric Statistics (SLDS) 2020
    • Cancelled due to the ongoing COVID19 pandemic
  • Conference on Statistical Learning and Data Science (SLDS) 2024
    • Newport Beach, California, November 5 - 8, 2024
